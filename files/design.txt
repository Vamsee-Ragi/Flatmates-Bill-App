Title:

Flatmates Bill

Description:

An app that takes as input the amount of bill for a particular time period and the number of days
that each of the flatmate has stayed in the house during the time period and returns how much each flatmate has
to pay. it also generates a PDF report stating the names of the flatmates, the time period and how much each has
to pay.

Objects:

    Bill :
        amount
        period
    Flatmate:
        name
        days in house
        pays(bill)
    PDF Report:
        filename
        generate(flatmate1, flatmate2)
